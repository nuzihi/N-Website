Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nuzihi?
Nuzihi is an initiative that was started By Young Tech ethusiasts to try and curb the challenge of young people feeling ashamed and uncomfortable with traditional therapy i.e. Seeing therapists face to face. This initiative thus allows one to access all therapy yhey need from their mobile phone as well as learn more about the Common mental health issues.
How can I get involved?
You can join our community, attend our mental health events, or contribute to our open-source projects for the developers.
