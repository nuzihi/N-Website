Therapy Made Simple and Comfortable

Nuzihi is the solution to the fear of seeking therapy, talking to peers about our well-being ,accessing insightful resources on various mental health problems -signs and their management and much more!

Welcome to Nuzihi .We are an organization comprising University of Nairobi and other university students who are passionate on alleviating the rising rate of mental health illnesses among students both in the University and the other institutions in the country as well. We operate on a nonprofit basis and within an inspiration of the Standard Development Goal Number 3 which calls for Good Health and Well-being. And therefore sswe aim at Improving Mental Health amongst students in Universities, colleges and high schools specifically in urban institutions in Nairobi, Kiambu , Kajiado and its environs where the cases are rampant.

Our goal is in alignment with the Sustainable Development Goals ,goal number 3 Which calls for Good health and well-being

Healthy Minds, Healthy Lives!

We value and acknowledge the correlation between mental wellness and overall body health. This is why we equip you with features such as : Virtual environment to help you practice mindfulness, Live spaces to have conversations on your mental wellness and Nuzihi ai who listens and counsels

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote mental health and wellbeing in the community by creating a supportive environment and advocating for mental health policies and practices that promote the wellbeing of all people hence bridging the gap in place with access to quality mental health care.

Our Vision

To tap into tech capabilities in building a community that prioritizes mental health and wellbeing, where people feel safe and supported to discuss and seek help for mental health challenges without fear of stigma or discrimination.

Talk to Nuzihi

Nuzihi AI is a friend that supports upto 5 Languages i.e. English, Swahili,French ,German and Spanish.
She holds Voice enabled conversations with you about your well-being, gives suggestions if not solutions and enables you navigate the app

This feature is limited to Getting help on mental health matters and app navigation only!

Live spaces

These are spaces with peers who are enrolled to Wellness programs on Nuzihi.
You get to access chatrooms,and video forums. (Please note that the Video forums are optional)
The spaces will enable you to create your ideal support partner from the proffesionals on these spaces throughout your wellness journey

The spaces are assigned to people of same age group ,condition with two proffesionals to offer guide

Virtual Environment

This is a virtual reality setting that allows you to select from the available relaxation atmospheres to help you relax
Select from tranquil atmospheres necessary fro your meditation,sleep and anxiety control

This feature might require third party applications for use

Our Team

We have a Passionate team that is focused on turning the dream of Healthy minds, Healthy lives a reality .

As the Founder of Nuzihi, I have witnessed firsthand the profound impact that technology can have on mental health. In a world where the barriers to accessing quality mental health care are all too real, technology stands as a beacon of hope, offering innovative solutions that transcend geographical, financial, and social constraints. Our commitment to integrating technology into mental health services has not only expanded our reach but has also enhanced the effectiveness of our interventions. From virtual therapy sessions that connect individuals to professionals regardless of location, to AI-driven tools that provide personalized mental health insights, we are at the forefront of a digital revolution in mental health care. The journey of mental wellness is deeply personal, yet universally relevant. It is a path that requires compassion, understanding, and continuous support. Through our technological endeavors, we strive to ensure that no one has to walk this path alone. We believe in empowering individuals with the resources they need to take charge of their mental well-being, and in doing so, we are creating a future where mental health care is accessible, stigma-free, and centered around the unique needs of each individual. Join us as we continue to break down barriers and build bridges in mental health care, harnessing the power of technology to create a world where mental wellness is a reality for all.
