Partnerships and Donations

Nuzihi is the solution to the fear of seeking therapy, talking to peers about our well-being ,accessing insightful resources on various mental health problems -signs and their management and much more!

Welcome to Nuzihi .We are an organization comprising University of Nairobi and other university students who are passionate on alleviating the rising rate of mental health illnesses among students both in the University and the other institutions in the country as well. We operate on a nonprofit basis and within an inspiration of the Standard Development Goal Number 3 which calls for Good Health and Well-being. And therefore sswe aim at Improving Mental Health amongst students in Universities, colleges and high schools specifically in urban institutions in Nairobi, Kiambu , Kajiado and its environs where the cases are rampant.

Our goal is in alignment with the Sustainable Development Goals ,goal number 3 Which calls for Good health and well-being
